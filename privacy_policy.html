Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 2026

📋 Short version: Cyber Bird does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Cyber Bird: Phoenix Evolution does not collect any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Payment or financial information

2. How the Game Works

Cyber Bird is a fully offline, single-player arcade game. All game data (your high score, evolution stage, current progress) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

The game does not require an internet connection to play.

3. Third-Party Services

Cyber Bird does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media platforms. There are no in-app purchases and no ads.

4. Children's Privacy

Cyber Bird is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any data, there are no special considerations needed for users under 13. We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations.

5. Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is your local game progress (high score), which is saved on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

support@cyberbird.app

We aim to respond within 48 hours.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. You are always in full control of your game data, which can be removed by deleting the app from your device.

🎮 Cyber Bird is committed to a simple, private gaming experience. No data. No ads. Just play.
